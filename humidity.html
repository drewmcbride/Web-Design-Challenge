Humidity

The humidity data was particularly hard to pick a pattern from because many of the cities had 100% humidity. Because of this, we were unable to make definitive conclusions regarding correlations between latitude and humidity. In future studies it could be worth specifically sampling for cities without 100% humidity to see if a relationship is present.
